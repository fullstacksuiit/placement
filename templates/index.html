Developing skills

Encouraging the students and providing them with eminent classes that help them develop not only their technical skills but also managerial, analytical, interpersonal, communicative and creative skills. These skills make them superlative, makes them to think out the box and stand out in the crowd.

Interdisciplinary Model

Interdisciplinary learning is maximized when faculty from different disciplines work together to help students make meaningful connections between different subject areas. Making cognitive connections between different concepts is very beneficial. This helps students develop and apply critical thinking skills.

Proficient Faculty

The focus on student-centred learning over the past years has marginalized teachers in research and methodology. Our teachers are a combination of expert and didactical knowledge, practical teaching experience, skills that have been learnt and practised, insights and attitudes, above all with respect to interaction with students they are praiseworthy.

Our Courses

The institute has adequate faculty members who are competent in their respective fields of specialization. The institute also ropes in the experienced faculty of the Sambalpur University and has visitors from National Institutes like IITs/ISI/NITs/Central Universities/Software Companies to expose students to accomplished scholars. Students are involved in multi-institutional projects and internships funded by Govt. agencies and collaborate sector.
